Privacy Policy - Daily Quran Verses

Last updated: November 8, 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data in the Daily Quran Verses Chrome extension.

Information Collection

We collect minimal data necessary for the extension's functionality:

  • Current verse position
  • Reading progress
  • Notification preferences

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not collect or transmit any personal information to external servers.

API Usage

We use the public Quran API (jsdelivr.net) to fetch verses and translations. No personal data is sent to this API.

Permissions

The extension requires these permissions:

  • Storage: To save your reading progress
  • Notifications: For verse reminders
  • Alarms: For scheduling reminders

Data Security

We implement security best practices to protect your data. All data remains on your local device.

Updates

This privacy policy may be updated. Check this page for any changes.

Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
